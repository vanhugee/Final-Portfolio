Who I Am

I am senior at Davidson College majoring in Computer Science with a passion for software development and cloud technologies. I enjoy tackling complex challenges, whether it’s through collaborative team projects or contributing to open-source initiatives.

My Experience

I’ve developed a deep passion for containerization and automation technologies like Kubernetes, Docker, and AWX. These tools have sparked my interest in building scalable, efficient systems, and I’ve enjoyed exploring how they can transform development and deployment workflows.

As a newcomer to Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), I have worked on a variety of design projects, including website design, data visualization, building VR worlds, and creating physical prototypes.
